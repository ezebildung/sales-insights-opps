Sales Insights

Week of June 23, 2026 · 7 calls reviewed

Insights

Competitor Mautic (open-source email) is the most common email incumbent, cited for complexity and maintenance.
Objection OneSignal's $19/month headline creates false anchors; real per-MAU cost shocked multiple prospects.
Use Case Consolidating separate email + push tools into OneSignal is a recurring driver in multiple deals.
Objection Committee budget approval is a recurring blocker; champions need exec-ready decks to close deals.
Doubt MAU definition and overage mechanics required lengthy explanations mid-pitch in nearly every call.

Opportunities & Learnings

Free-plan deprecation and August price increase are strong urgency levers — surface them earlier, not reactively.
Internal champions need committee-ready proposal decks, not just quotes, for senior decision-makers.
Walk through MAU calculation and overage scenarios live during pricing to prevent sticker shock.
Leading with a structured methodology (planning framework, event taxonomy) strongly differentiates.
Local-language WhatsApp support vs. OneSignal's US-only support is an underused differentiator.

Product Gaps

No native WhatsApp channel — Twilio still required as intermediary; native Meta integration pending.
Segments only support rolling date windows, not fixed ranges — breaks cross-tool audience parity.
No native in-app survey builder — in-app messages can't collect structured feedback from users.
