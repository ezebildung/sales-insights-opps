Insights

Opportunities & Learnings

Show the website vs. contract price gap proactively — letting leads discover it themselves mid-call creates confusion and friction.
Define MAUs explicitly at the top of every call — the misunderstanding came up in 3 different calls this week.
Introduce the Events feature during discovery, not just late-stage demos — one lead rethought their entire integration after seeing it.
Use Salesforce's unpredictable billing as a contrast anchor whenever it appears in the lead's current stack.
Free plan deprecation is driving inbound from stagnant accounts — lean on this urgency actively in outreach and openers.

Product Gaps

No native segment filter for push opt-in status — must be sent as a custom tag from the SDK to be usable in journeys.
No native WhatsApp channel — requires Twilio webhook + separate Meta billing, which creates pricing confusion with leads.
