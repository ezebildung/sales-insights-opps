Week of June 22, 2026 · 6 calls reviewed

Insights

Use Case Transactional push (card payment alerts) is the primary use case for fintech clients
Objection Sticker shock jumping from free to paid (~$600/mo); clients need ROI framing to close
Competitor Salesforce dominates marketing stack for mid-market; unpredictable billing is their main pain
Use Case Abandoned-cart/loan journeys are the top automation priority for financial services prospects
Doubt Clients consistently misunderstand MAU definition — conflating it with opted-in or messaged users

Opportunities & Learnings

Explain the free-to-paid price jump early in discovery to prevent late-stage sticker shock
Ask about existing WhatsApp/SMS setup upfront — webhook value depends on providers already in use
Lead with Events feature in demos — tag-only clients miss high-value automation patterns
Qualify internal approval timelines before presenting pricing to avoid deals stalling late
Use Health Score and PLG metrics framing when prospects are in SLG-to-PLG transitions

Product Gaps

No native dashboard filter to distinguish opted-in from opted-out users
WhatsApp is not a native channel — requires webhook workaround to third-party providers
No cross-team send visibility — teams can send overlapping messages with no unified user send log
