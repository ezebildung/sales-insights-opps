Sales Insights

Week of 22 Jun 2026 · 7 calls reviewed

Insights

Competitor Salesforce is the most cited alternative, mentioned by two prospects (Creditel and Proa) for email/WhatsApp automation and as a general messaging platform with unpredictable pricing.
Objection Pricing expectation mismatch: prospects expect significant discounts when negotiating lower MAU tiers, but OneSignal's pricing doesn't scale down proportionally below 25K MAUs.
Objection Budget approval through committee or CEO-level sign-off is adding deal cycle length. At least two prospects need directorship buy-in before committing, with no pre-assigned budget.
Use Case Abandoned cart / incomplete loan application recovery is the most common use case across financial services leads (Creditel, Proa), replacing manual follow-up processes taking 24h+.
Use Case Habit creation and re-engagement (not e-commerce conversion) emerged as the primary use case for a fitness app prospect (MudeFit), requiring a different demo script framing.
Doubt WhatsApp integration complexity is a repeated blocker. Prospects assume OneSignal is a direct Meta Business Partner and are confused by the Webhook approach.
Other A Mixpanel discovery call (AtlasGov) showed strong PLG demand from B2B SaaS companies transitioning from Sales-Led to Product-Led Growth, with HubSpot as primary downstream integration.

Opportunities & Learnings

The WhatsApp objection needs a standardized response. A one-slide explainer on "how WhatsApp works in OneSignal today vs. what's coming" would reduce friction and manage expectations proactively.
For financial services demos, prepare a fintech-specific template in advance (loan application events, credit eligibility tags). The current generic demo resonates but takes too long to contextualize.
Pricing conversations benefit from early transparency. When MAU tiers go down, proactively explain that pricing doesn't change materially below 25K MAUs — before the prospect discovers it at proposal review.
For deals going to committee approval, qualify the economic buyer and pre-assigned budget in the discovery call. This saves time and helps frame urgency around the October free plan deadline.
Build a dedicated "non-transactional engagement" talk track for apps like fitness, media, and education. MudeFit showed this adaptation can work well — it just needs to be faster and more consistent.
