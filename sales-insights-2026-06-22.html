Opportunities & Learnings

The MAU definition should be proactively explained in every pricing conversation — it's a recurring source of confusion and sticker shock.
When a lead has budget resistance, anchoring to the cost of inaction (manual ops, missed conversions) is more effective than discounting.
WhatsApp is a blocker in multiple deals — having a clear, repeatable answer on the webhook/Twilio/native roadmap would reduce friction.
Calls with technical stakeholders (devs/PMs) move faster when custom events and SDK methods are shown early — it shifts the conversation from "can it?" to "how."
For free-plan upgrades, framing the Oct 1 deadline as a hard cutoff (not a limit) earlier in the cycle creates urgency without feeling like pressure.
