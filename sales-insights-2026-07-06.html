Opportunities & Learnings

Pricing is rarely quoted live, forcing a second touch by email instead of closing momentum.
Calls often reach a technical contact instead of the actual budget-approving decision-maker.
A parent company's reaction to the pricing change can spread to sibling accounts.
Reps pitch full platform breadth before confirming the lead's specific KPI, diluting focus.
Accounts going quiet after a stalled POC aren't proactively followed up before they self-solve.

Product Gaps

No native WhatsApp channel — only usable via webhook integration to the Meta API.
Can't infer demographic attributes like gender — only tracks behavior or explicit data.
No lightweight way to schedule one-off dated campaign batches without building a full Journey.
Can't scope segment access to sub-accounts (e.g. per distributor or per corporate client).
No built-in funnel/drop-off analytics — flow completion tracking requires an external tool.
